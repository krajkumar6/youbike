FAQ

1.How does Youbike work?
Step 1: Customer books an appointment with us by email for service
Step 2: Our technician will contact you to confirm the appointment
Step 3: Technician will reach your residence and service the bike at your residence
Step 4: Technician hands over the key back to you and collects the money

2.How will you water wash the bike?
We will not be able to water wash the bike because we don’t have the infrastructure to clean the vehicle at your doorstep.

3.What is your USP?
Our technicians are industry professionals or industry trained.We bring service to your home at your convenience.We use only genuine spares

4.What is your rate?
Labor - ₹300 for scooters, ₹450 for bikes. The spares/consumables are extra. We charge consumables/spares on actuals so there’s no margin for us on spares.

5.What if I don’t want to change a spare?
Our technician will not change the spare(eg- brake shoe,clutch cable) if you don’t want to, but will share the recommendations to you.

6.Are you open on all days?
Since we are beginning operations we take appointments only on weekends.

7.What brands do you service?
Honda,Hero,TVS, Yamaha,Bajaj & Suzuki

8.What is NOT covered in your service?
We are focussed only on preventive maintenance so we dont take up jobs that require to be done in workshops. eg:extensive body shop repair/damage jobs etc. We also do not provide water wash services at the moment
